# [[ Mental Well-being ]]
# TIPS: Practice daily mindfulness or meditation (5-10 mins). Listen to calming music playlists (e.g., classical, ambient). Read books on motherhood or light fiction. Journal your thoughts and feelings.
# SUPPORT: Stay connected with friends, family, or a support group for expectant mothers. Communicate your feelings with your partner.
# BOOKS: "What to Expect When You're Expecting", "The Whole-Brain Child".
# MUSIC: Search for "Calm Classical" or "Peaceful Piano" playlists on streaming services.

# [[ Doctor Checkups ]]
# REMINDERS: Regular checkups are vital. Typically: once a month for weeks 4-28, twice a month for weeks 28-36, and weekly from week 36 to birth.

# [[ SOS / Emergency ]]
# PROTOCOL: If you experience severe symptoms like heavy bleeding, severe abdominal pain, or a sudden loss of fluid, this is an emergency.
# ACTION: Immediately contact your doctor, your local emergency number (e.g., 911), or go to the nearest hospital.
