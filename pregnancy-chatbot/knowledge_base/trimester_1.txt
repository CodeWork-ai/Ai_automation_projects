# [[ Trimester 1: Foundations ]]
# DIET: Focus on folate-rich foods (lentils, spinach). Eat small, frequent meals to combat nausea. Stay hydrated. Ginger tea can help with morning sickness.
# EXERCISE: Gentle activity is key. Focus on walking, prenatal yoga, and swimming. Avoid high-impact sports. Listen to your body and rest often.
# DAILY ROUTINE: Prioritize sleep (aim for 8-10 hours). Take prenatal vitamins as prescribed.
